## WHY

Provides an opportunity for BD and P&S to review active engagements, coordinate delivery target, and reduce delivery risks.

## HOW

1. Review Asana [Active Engagements](https://app.asana.com/0/portfolio/1211914502801258/1211914511709245) portfolio
2. Prioritize `Off track` and `At risk` engagements.
3. Review Accounts Receivables in SAGE for invoices that are [60 days past due](https://www-p504.intacct.com/ia/acct/frameset.phtml?.sess=FYzzJWjj2l2P4bX6IqN_iGgzXY4lVBWM3vONVBTLj_G1_iKjfpUKUVyM&.cc=spZoEP-NR951EoqjuDbpcOTVX3DobQ45K0XiKz52vKE.).

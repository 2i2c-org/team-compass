# Monthly accounting and budget projection process

Each month, we update our [budget projections](accounting:projections) with the latest accounting data.
This is a two-step process:

1. **CS&S notifies us that the books are closed** for the previous month in SAGE Intacct.
2. **CS&S updates the revenue/expenses** in our [runway model and projections sheet](accounting:projections) with the latest actuals.

We then use this as an opportunity to:

- Confirm that our accounting transactions look correct.
- Flag any items that require extra attention, such as contracts that will expire soon.
- Update our financial projections and calculate a new runway for 2i2c.

For the detailed process and all key links, see the [budget projections page](accounting:projections).

# Reporting

1. The system of reporting Community Enablement hours should be applicable across the entire 2i2c team.
    1. A method to track all Community Enablement commitments.
    2. A method to track all Community Enablement hours delivered.
    3. Key fields to track:
      - Engagement (service agreement) to which this consulting activity is allocated.
      - Name of the person delivering the Community Enablement activity
      - Number of hours delivered (in units of hours)
      - Description of the Community Enablement activity. Details of the activity should be proportional to the number of consulting hours delivered. If it was 1 hour of consulting, it might be “attended the weekly community meeting”. If it was 24 hours of consulting activity, a two page report may be more appropriate.
